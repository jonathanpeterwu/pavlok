Meet Pavlok



The Habit Forming Device

Pavlok is the first bracelet designed to actually change your habits, and not just measure what you already
do. Pavlok, designed by Behavioral Technologies, integrates what you do with who
you want to become --- and helps you actually achieve the goals you want to achieve.





Why Build Pavlok

We're building Pavlok to change your daily life

We at Behaviorial Technologies are building Pavlok, the first of its kind wearable device that goes beyond tracking your daily routines, and acting upon your daily routines. We're a company focused on design, behavioral science, and lifehacking to help improve your life. We believe that with the right mix of technology, behavior modification, and incentives, we can help you change any of your habits.
